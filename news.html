The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) at Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology (MBCET) was established in 2013. Its mission is to inspire students to pursue entrepreneurial passions by equipping them with essential technical and non-technical skills. Catalyst, the IEDC at MBCET, emphasizes instilling an entrepreneurial spirit, promoting self-learning, and empowering students to develop solutions for real-world problems.
